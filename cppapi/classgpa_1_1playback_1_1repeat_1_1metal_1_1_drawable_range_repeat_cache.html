class gpa::playback::repeat::metal::DrawableRangeRepeatCache : public gpa::playback::repeat::RangeRepeatCache

Tracks state for MTLDrawables and recreates them.

The biggest challenge for tracking MTLDrawable is tracking its presented state and lifetime. MTLDrawables cannot be reused and must be released appropriately.
